# Baseline: comprehension-production gap, not a knowledge gap

Satyam self-reports strong English comprehension but weak spoken production, specifically across four
symptoms: clunky/awkward sentence construction, slow word retrieval, low fluency with frequent pauses, and
pronunciation clarity issues. This means lessons should assume vocabulary and grammar *knowledge* are largely
present — the bottleneck is real-time production and retrieval speed, not rules or words he's never seen.
Practice should prioritize spoken/retrieval exercises over passive explanation or vocabulary lists. Relevant
contexts named: work meetings/presentations, everyday conversation, debate/persuasion, storytelling. See
[[MISSION.md]].
