# Formal/meeting register is harder than casual conversation for PREP retrieval

After lesson 0002 (six worked PREP examples, three meeting + three conversation, plus two build-your-own
exercises), Satyam reported both were tough — expected, since the framework is only one lesson old — but
the **meeting/formal setting was noticeably harder than casual conversation**. Likely cause: meeting answers
require crisper tradeoff framing ("X days now vs. Y later") and professional phrasing that isn't yet
automatic, on top of the new PREP structure itself — two new loads at once, rather than one.

**Implication:** don't move to module 2 (retrieval speed) yet. Give meetings specifically one more pass —
but with a scaffold (a reusable phrase bank for each PREP beat in professional register) rather than just
more raw examples, so the vocabulary load is lighter while the structural retrieval is practiced. Conversation
register did not get flagged as hard, so it does not need another dedicated pass right now. See [[MISSION.md]]
and learning record [[0001-baseline-spoken-production-gap]].
