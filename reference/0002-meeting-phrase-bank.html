Meeting Phrase Bank

Interchangeable professional-register openers for each PREP beat — so wording isn't a second thing to invent mid-sentence.

Pick any phrase from a row — they're interchangeable within that beat. The goal is that the connector becomes automatic, so your attention goes to the content, not to sounding professional.
P — opening the point
  • "I think we should..."
  • "My recommendation is..."
  • "I'd go with..."
  • "Here's where I land: ..."
R — giving the reason
  • "...because..."
  • "...mainly due to..."
  • "The key factor is..."
  • "That's driven by..."
E — giving the example
  • "For example, ..."
  • "Case in point: ..."
  • "We saw this when..."
  • "To illustrate, ..."
P — closing the point
  • "So — ..."
  • "Bottom line: ..."
  • "Net-net, ..."
  • "Which is why ..."
